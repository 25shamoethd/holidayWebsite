Christmas Markets (Europe)

Festive markets with holiday foods, crafts, and entertainment are popular in places like Germany, Austria, and France.

Write Letters to Santa Claus

Children write letters to Santa and leave out cookies and milk for him, with some also leaving carrots for his reindeer.

Lighting Advent Candles

Some families light candles on an Advent wreath leading up to Christmas.

Hanging Stockings

Stockings are hung by the fireplace or on walls for Santa to fill with small treats and gifts.

Yule Logs (Scandinavia)

A traditional log burned in the hearth, now often symbolized by a Yule log cake.
